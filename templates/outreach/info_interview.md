# Informational interview ask (Dalton 6-point structure, <75 words email / <100 words LinkedIn)

Rules from the research:
- Ask for insight and advice about THEIR experience. Never job leads, never a referral.
- State the connection point in the first sentence (alumni 60-80% reply; mutual 30%+; their content 20%+).
- Ask for 15 minutes, phrased as a question ending in "?".
- More than half the words about them.
- If they say yes, reply same day with 3-4 concrete time slots (20-30% of yeses never schedule).

Proven shape:
"Hi [Name] - I'm [her name], [shared connection point]. May I ask you about your experience as [their role] at [Company]? I'm exploring [narrow interest] and [broader interest], and how you got there would be really useful to hear. Would you have 15 minutes in the next couple of weeks?"
