# Referral ask (50-150 words; ONLY after rapport - a chat, a real exchange)

Rules:
- "Would you feel comfortable referring me?" - the explicit out is mandatory.
- Do the work for them: job link + req ID + a 2-3 line paste-ready blurb + resume offer.
- Ask before or immediately after applying (referrals often can't attach later).
- Never to a stranger in message #1. If unsure, ask "anything you'd suggest before I apply?" and let them offer.

Shape:
"Hi [Name] - [Company] posted a [Job Title] role (ID [####]) that fits my background in [thing] well. Since we talked about [topic from your chat], I wanted to ask: would you feel comfortable referring me? Totally fine if not. I can send my resume and a short blurb to make it painless either way."
