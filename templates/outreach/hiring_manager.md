# Note to hiring manager after applying (<400 characters ideal)

Rules: only if the posting doesn't forbid contact. Expect ~40% reply on well-targeted ones.
RISE opener: lead with relevance to THEIR world, not her enthusiasm.

Shape:
1. "I noticed [team/company specific thing]" or "I just applied for [role] on your team."
2. One sentence of proof: the single strongest true match, with a real number if she has one.
3. Why their team specifically (one honest reason).
4. Light ask: one answerable question, or "happy to share more if useful."

Don't: restate the resume, say "keeping my application fresh", flatter generically.
