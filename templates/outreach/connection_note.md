# Connection request note (max 300 characters, hard platform limit)

Structure (research: notes nearly double post-accept reply rates):
1. The shared hook first: alumni, mutual connection, their post, same field/city.
2. One honest sentence of why connecting.
3. Optional soft question. No pitch, no resume, no job ask. Ever.

Example shapes:
- "Hi [Name], we're both [shared thing]. I've been following your posts on [topic] and would like to stay in the loop. Open to connecting?"
- "Hi [Name], [Mutual] mentioned your work on [thing]. I work in [field] too and would love to follow along."
