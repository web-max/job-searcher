# Internal recruiter intro (<400 characters - they triage in ~6 seconds)

Rules: name the specific req (title + req ID). Recruiters ignore "can you find me a role?"
Make her look like an easy submit for an open req.

Shape:
"Hi [Name] - I just applied for [Job Title] (req [####]). Quick context: I'm a [current role] with [X yrs] in [most relevant thing], and [one metric matching the req]. [If true: '[Referrer] suggested I flag it to you.'] Anything useful I can send over?"

Timing: right after applying; one follow-up after ~5 business days.
