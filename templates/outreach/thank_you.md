# Thank-you after a call (within 24 hours)

Rules (this is what converts an adviser into an advocate):
- Name one specific thing they said that helped.
- Name the action she already took on their advice (read the thing, contacted the person).
- Promise to keep them posted - then actually do it 1-2 weeks later.

Shape:
"Hi [Name], thanks again for yesterday. Your point about [specific thing] changed how I'm thinking about [topic] - I already [action taken]. I'll let you know how it goes. Really appreciate the time."
